How it works

Step 1:

Download Telegram from App Store or Google Play Before you can begin your MeCall.io journey, you'll need to have Telegram installed on your device. Telegram is a cloud-based messaging app renowned for its speed and security, making it a popular choice for both personal and professional communication. It's available for download on both the App Store and Google Play, so whether you're an iOS or Android user, you're covered. A quick search and tap on "Install" will have you ready in no time.

Step 2: Open Telegram on Your Phone

Once Telegram is installed, open the app and set up your account if you haven't done so already. The setup process is straightforward: enter your phone number, verify it with a code sent via SMS, and you're in. After completing these steps, you'll find yourself on the Telegram home screen, which is your gateway to connecting with the world.

Step 3: Search for MeCall.io

With Telegram up and running, it's time to find MeCall.io. Using the search bar at the top of your Telegram home screen, type in "MeCall.io" and hit search. This will pull up the MeCall.io bot.

Step 4: Open the MeCall.io Bot and Click on the MeCall Button

Once you've located the MeCall.io bot, open the chat and take a moment to familiarize yourself with its interface. You'll notice a "MeCall" button prominently displayed—a simple click on the button initiates the process.
